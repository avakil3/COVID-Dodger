Instructions

  • Welcome to COVID Dodger! The objective of the game is to direct the player around the screen while navigating a slew of COVID-infected humans.
  • Control the character using the arrow keys and maintain a 6-feet distance from incoming people to avoid infection
  • The longer the player survives, the higher the score will be
  • As time progresses, the difficulty of the game will rise as more infected people will move across the screen
  • Be sure to get a covid vaccine shot when it appears on the screen! The player will receive immunity for 10 seconds.
