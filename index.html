Instructions

  • Welcome to COVID Dodger!
  • COVID Dodger is a javascript-based game where the goal is to direct the main character around while navigating a slew of COVID-infected humans.
  • The player must control a character and maintain a 6-feet distance from incoming people to avoid infection. The longer the player survives, the higher the score will be.
  • As time progresses, the difficulty of the game will rise as more infected people will move across the screen more quickly with more random switching of direction.
  • Be sure to get a covid vaccine shot when it appears on the screen! The player will receive immunity for 10 seconds.
